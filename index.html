About Finn

I help leaders see what their teams are truly capable of.

I'm Finn Breland — a team and leadership development consultant based in the Nashville area. I hold a Master’s degree in Information Technology Management, and my background spans both the educational and corporate sectors, where I’ve spent years navigating complex organizations, leading teams, and learning firsthand what makes them succeed or struggle.

As a certified Working Genius instructor, I bring a structured, evidence-based approach to understanding how people naturally work — and how leaders can use that knowledge to build teams where everyone thrives.

My approach is shaped by Patrick Lencioni's Five Dysfunctions framework: I believe healthy conflict, real trust, and honest communication aren't soft skills — they're competitive advantages.

  • Certified Working Genius Facilitator & Instructor
  • Deep experience with mid-size organizational teams
  • Based in Franklin, TN — serving greater Middle Tennessee
  • Passionate about putting people in position to succeed

Our Approach

Healthy teams don't happen by accident. They're built with intention.

We believe the foundation of every high-performing team is the same: self-awareness, honest communication, and leaders who put people in position to succeed.

Step 01

Discover the Gifts

Using the Working Genius framework, we help every team member understand what kind of work energizes them — and what drains them. This awareness alone changes everything.

Step 02

Build Real Trust

When people are honest about their strengths and frustrations, teams stop performing and start connecting. Trust is built through vulnerability, not just results.

Step 03

Unleash the Team

We equip your team with tangible toolkits on team culture, meeting etiquette, and team strategy and planning — practical resources your team can implement right away.
